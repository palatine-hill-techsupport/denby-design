Home Services Audit App

The Why: Our team invested considerable time and effort into conducting surveys for many of the jobs completed by our subcontractors across Australia. However, there was no clear process or policy for reviewing these jobs. Each person approached their work in their own way, resulting in data that was often unusable. As a result, the management team never checked or used it, making it a big waste of time.

The How: We needed to establish a standard that all stakeholders could agree on. Fortunately, there was a pre-existing rate card that was regularly negotiated between the management team and the subcontractors. This card included very clear, mandatory minimums for every service, which became the foundation for the first version of the web app.

The Outcome: The unified data that the tool generated was a hit, and the actionable statistics it provided could now be used for meaningful reporting. However, the feedback from the team mostly focused on the UI. When a new cohort was hired, they wanted some UI adjustments to make their UX more of a learning experience as well. So, we made those changes and even added a ‚ÄúPro Mode Toggle‚Äù to keep the old UI as an option.

Ultimately, the success of this project was so significant that our Salesforce admin adopted my code and consultations to build the audit tool within Salesforce itself. It‚Äôs now a fully integrated feature in our daily workflow, providing real, usable data.
