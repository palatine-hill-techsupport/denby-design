Work With Me

      
Freelance work, full-time roles, weird little product problems, and projects that need a bit more UX in the room.

    


    


      


        
I help teams make digital products a bit less cooked. That might mean untangling a confusing flow, pressure-testing an idea before it becomes expensive, or bringing a bit more UX thinking into the way a product actually gets built.


        
I’m useful for:

        
    
          
  • Audit: Finding the friction, dead ends, weird assumptions, and “why is it like this?” moments.
    • 
          
  • Strategy: Turning messy needs, business pressure, and user problems into something the team can actually work with.
    • 
          
  • Design: Creating flows, interfaces, and prototypes that make sense before they become someone’s daily problem.
    • 
          
  • AI & workflow: Using AI where it helps clear the sludge, not where it replaces thinking.
    • 
          
  • Delivery support: Helping ideas survive the trip from Figma to the real world.
